❓ Frequently Asked Questions

When is the GTA 6 release date?
GTA 6 is expected to release in 2026 for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S. Rockstar Games has not confirmed a specific date. The original 2025 target was pushed back during development.
What platforms will GTA 6 be on?
GTA 6 launches on PS5 and Xbox Series X/S. A PC version has not been officially confirmed — historically Rockstar releases on console first with PC following 12–18 months later.
Where is GTA 6 set?
GTA 6 is set in Vice City — Rockstar's fictional Miami — and the broader state of Leonida, inspired by Florida. The map includes swamps, beaches, rural highways, and a large urban core.
Can you play as a woman in GTA 6?
Yes! Lucia is the first playable female protagonist in a mainline GTA game. She and Jason are both fully playable throughout the story.
Will GTA 6 have online multiplayer?
An online mode is widely expected given GTA Online's success since 2013, but Rockstar has not announced GTA 6 Online officially yet.
How big is the GTA 6 map?
Not officially confirmed, but trailers show a much larger and more diverse environment than GTA V's Los Santos and Blaine County combined.
